our story

Eyespike started in 2002, and our story has really just begun. From our humble beginnings as a web design and development company to becoming a critical and strategic partner with CEUFast, Inc. We continue to build our brands and customer loyalty through a slow and steady nurturing of our core values that include honesty, hard work, and trust.

  1. April 2002

    Eyespike Design is founded in Lake City, Florida.

  2. June 2002

    Completed client projects including custom designs for First Federal Bank of Florida, Ellianos Coffee, and CEUFast.com.

  3. Noevember 2002

    Started planning and development of the next version of CEUFast.com, a platform for providing continuing education course to Nurses.

  4. October 2003

    Launched version 2 of CEUFast.com with upgraded service structure and new user interface design.

  5. October 2004

    Began full-time responsibility for CEUFast.com customer support.

  6. January 2005

    Established a strategic partnership with CEUFast, Inc to manage, market, develop and maintain CEUFast.com.

  7. April 2005

    Started planning and design concepts for CEUFast.com version 3.

  8. November 2005

    Created SpringsRUs.com website for the Columbia County Tourist Development council to promote tourism of the fresh water springs in and near North Central Florida.

  9. December 2005

    Increased Gross Revenue for CEUFast.com by 60% within the first year of our Marketing and Development Strategic Partnership.

  10. March 2006

    Launched NursingRequirements.com, a website that maintains a resource of Nurse licensing renewal dates, CE requirements, and links to official state websites.

  11. March 2006

    Won 10 Addy Awards at Annual AdFed of Gainesville Award Gala

  12. October 2007

    Awarded contract to Develop and Design a new website for First Federal Bank of Florida.

  13. February 2008

    Launched New version of FFSB.com (First Federal Savings Bank)

  14. September 2009

    Identity Package and Web Design for DropTop Customs

  15. March 2010

    SEMA Display Designs for Convertible Builders aka DropTop Customs

  16. January 2011

    Restructured company to focus solely on managing CEUFast and related web properties to create stronger brands and better user experiences.

  17. May 2012

    First Superstar Employee Hired! Jeff Thompson is our new Leada Developer and brings over 25 years of experience to the company!

What We Do

Define Strategies

To create a plan of action that will limit risks and provide the best chance for a positive outcome, every project needs careful consideration through research and experience. From the the seemingly mundane details, like choosing a domain name, to coordinating a year long nationwide marketing campaign, we pay close attention to every detail.

Build Brands

A well defined brand should effectively communicate who you are and what you do in a memorable way. Your Brand is more than just a name or logo, it establishes a customer’s expectations, defines the perceived value of a your products or services, and even who they are most likely to appeal.

Create Experiences

Every interaction with your company helps define an experience and an opportunity to build up or tear down your defined strategies or brand. We nurture every aspect of our customer’s experience and see the results of that through customer loyalty.
