our story

We continue to build our brands and customer loyalty through a slow and steady nurturing of our core values that include honesty, hard work, and trust.

Customer Service Champion

Job Description

Job Type: Customer Service Representative for CEUFast.com

Location: Remote/Work at Home

Hours: 9am to 6pm (one 60min or two 30min breaks)

Pay: First 90 Day (Temporary Full Time) - $9 per hour - Leading to $10 per hour plus Health Insurance after 90 days or after completing in-service training program.

Bonus Opportunities for handling after hours support cases.

Entry level position that can lead to a management level position in future for the right candidate.

Description

We are looking for a Customer Service Representative to ensure customers have the best possible experience using CEUFast.com. This individual will be responsible for answering emails and returning customer’s calls. Primarily interactions with customers will be online and will require strong communication and grammar skills with the ability to type a minimum of 30 wpm with 95% accuracy. The candidate will thoroughly document the progress and outcome of each support interaction/case. They will also need to be able to cross promote products, assist in customer sales, and help maintain support information documents.

We strive to provide our users with the best possible experience in using CEUFast.com and other websites that we manage. The candidate will need to be patient and enjoy helping customers resolve their issues in a timely manner.

We are a fast moving web based service company with customers throughout the United States and Puerto Rico. The candidate will need to be able to adapt to changes both in our website and in the changing requirements they will need to provide to our customers on a daily basis.

This is a Remote/Work at Home position and is available immediately.

Qualifications

  • Computer Savvy (familiarity with social media like twitter, facebook, and google+, and standard office applications like Word, Excel or Google Docs.)
  • Great teaching skills (people like to learn from you)
  • Ability to type 30 wpm or higher
  • A dedicated home office with a quiet work environment
  • Past experience in customer service or call center but we are willing to train the right candidate
  • Bi-lingual (Spanish) a big plus
